6.10.1 同? 􏷇? 冃? 􏷅?üîó‚Ñπ

同
ideograph

  connotation : equal always
  originates from : 同
  originally means : equal, as same as

冃
ideograph

  connotation : simply equal
  originates from : 冃
  originally means : hat
Ignore the original meaning in Chinese, and especially take it as the simplification version of 同 in Ming. (Do not confuse with 月)
􏷇 : 𭕄 + 同
ideograph

  connotation : equal without mutable
Almost same as 同 other than that 𭕄 implies difference about mutable data.

􏷅 : 冃 + 米
ideograph

  connotation : only equal on numbers
Almost same as 冃 other than that 米 implies difference about numbers.

Examples:
> (同? (􏽀 #\a #\b #\c) (􏽀 #\a #\b #\c))

#t

> (同? (􏽁 #\a #\b #\b) (􏽁 #\a #\b #\b))

#t

> (同? (􏿴 1 2) (􏿴 1 2))

#t

> (同? (expt 2 100) (expt 2 100))

#t
